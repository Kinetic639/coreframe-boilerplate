# Ambra — Public Pages Redesign Brief

Use this document to redesign the public-facing pages (homepage, features, pricing, auth) of **Ambra**, a SaaS warehouse management platform. The goal is a modern, content-rich design that replaces current placeholder content with real product information.

---

## 1. Product Overview

**App name:** Ambra  
**Type:** B2B SaaS — Warehouse & Inventory Management Platform  
**Primary language:** Polish (pl), secondary: English (en)  
**Target market:** Polish SME to enterprise businesses — warehouse managers, logistics coordinators, operations directors, shop owners with physical stock

**Core promise:**  
Ambra is a complete SaaS platform for warehouse and inventory management. Real-time product tracking, location management, QR code labels, stock movements, team collaboration — everything in one place.

**Primary tagline:**

> "Twój start z SaaS łatwiejszy niż kiedykolwiek"  
> _(Your SaaS start easier than ever)_

**Secondary tagline:**

> "Gotowy na rewolucję w zarządzaniu magazynem?"  
> _(Ready for a revolution in warehouse management?)_

**Value proposition bullets:**

- Zarządzaj zapasami w czasie rzeczywistym _(Manage inventory in real time)_
- Generuj kody QR i etykiety _(Generate QR codes and labels)_
- Śledź ruchy magazynowe _(Track stock movements)_
- Automatyzuj inwentaryzację _(Automate inventory audits)_
- Kontroluj dostęp zespołu _(Control team access)_
- Integruj z e-commerce _(Integrate with e-commerce: Shopify, WooCommerce, Allegro)_

## 4. How It Works (3-step flow)

Use for a "How it works" section:

1. **Skonfiguruj organizację** _(Set up your organization)_  
   Utwórz organizację, dodaj oddziały i zaproś zespół. Skonfiguruj role i uprawnienia w kilka minut.

2. **Dodaj produkty i lokalizacje** _(Add products and locations)_  
   Zdefiniuj strukturę magazynu, dodaj produkty z wariantami i przypisz do lokalizacji. Wydrukuj etykiety QR.

3. **Zarządzaj w czasie rzeczywistym** _(Manage in real time)_  
   Rejestruj przyjęcia, wydania i przesunięcia. Monitoruj stany, planuj audyty, analizuj raporty.

## 7. Design Direction

### Colors

| Context             | Color         | Hex       |
| ------------------- | ------------- | --------- |
| Primary / Brand     | Emerald green | `#10b981` |
| Warehouse module    | Emerald green | `#10b981` |
| Teams module        | Purple        | `#8b5cf6` |
| Organization module | Indigo        | `#6366f1` |
| Tools module        | Amber         | `#f59e0b` |
| Danger / Admin      | Red           | `#dc2626` |

**Suggested overall palette:**  
Dark navy/slate background for hero, white cards, emerald green primary CTA, gray-900 body text. Support dark mode.

### Typography feel

- Headlines: Bold, large, confident — warehouse/operations software that means business
- Body: Clean, readable — professional B2B SaaS

### UI style

- Modern SaaS (think: Linear, Vercel, Notion)
- Clean white sections alternating with subtle gray/dark sections
- Rounded cards with subtle shadows
- Pill badges for plan labels ("Popularny", "14 dni próbnych")
- Smooth gradients in hero and CTA sections
- Browser-frame mockups for app screenshots
- Animated counters for stats (e.g., "98% dokładność inwentarza")

### Tone of voice

- Professional but approachable
- Focused on efficiency, time savings, reliability
- Polish-first (primary language is Polish)
- Business outcomes, not technical jargon

## 10. Tech Notes for the Builder

- **Framework:** Next.js (App Router) with TypeScript
- **Auth routes:** Already implemented — link to `/sign-up` and `/sign-in`
- **i18n:** The app supports Polish (`pl`) and English (`en`). Public pages use `[locale]` prefix. Default locale is Polish.
- **Existing UI kit:** shadcn/ui + Tailwind CSS — if building inside the repo, use these primitives
- **Do not redesign:** Dashboard, admin panel, or any `/dashboard/*` routes
- **QR/scanner routes:** `/qr/[token]` — not a public marketing page, skip
- **Public maps:** `/maps/[branchId]` — warehouse map viewer, not a marketing page, skip
